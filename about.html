About Us

We turn ideas into art.


For each project, we establish relationships with partners who we know will help us create added value for your project. As well as bringing together the public and private sectors, we make sector-overarching links to gather knowledge and to learn from each other. The way we undertake projects is based on permanently applying values that reinforce each other. Values such as socio-cultural value, experimental value, building-technical value and economical value. This way of working allows us to raise our clients' projects to a higher level.

Our Services



Land Scape

Landscaping is a herculean task whether it includes revamping a neighbourhood or formulating a base plan for a city. Our team at Edge Architects consists of the most skilled and proficient planners, designers, architects and mobility engineers who strive to create and develop for our clients a dynamic, resilient, innovative and sustainable environment. Our approach focuses on the transformative and progressive changes that shape our environments.

Architecture

We at Edge Architects believe that the best of buildings are obtained from an amalgamation of elements that add to the character and functionality of the structure, its location on the globe, its surrounding environment and its ability to inspire and make an impact in the field of architecture. Our team of well-experienced and skilled architects, civil engineers and designers work together and to their fullest potential to give you the most creative and sustainable designs present in the market today.

Interiors

At Edge Architects we believe an elegant interior is just as vital as a sophisticated exterior. The initial step for the creation of a striking interior is to develop an understanding of our client's ideas, the utilization of the space and lastly their sense of connection with the place to add a touch of personalization to it. We aim to present and create for our clients a place that is graceful, ingenious and functional, that compliments the building’s outward expression while evoking a sense of home in them.

Retrofit

Retrofitting is the essence of reducing the carbon footprint that the building environment has left on this earth. Our team here at Edge Architects come up with sustainable and innovative designs that help enhance and make the old, existing structures and designs resilient and adaptable to the present-day modern world. By working in collaboration we strive to give you designs with improved and sophisticated performance and functionality that also help in reducing the embodied carbon content.
