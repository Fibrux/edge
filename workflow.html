Workflow

#1. Greeting


First of all we will introduce our campany and give a brief explanation about the future flow and period.Pease tell us about your wishes while looking at cases so far.

#2. Field Survey


If the property has been decided the perons in charge will visit the site directly and check the dimentsions and equipment status of the tenant. At this stage, please tell us about your rough budget, schedule, and desired space image. At this time, it is also determined whether the installed capactiy is sufficient for the for the desired specifications.

#3. Layout Proposal


We will first collaborate with you on the layout before proposing the final design planning. Layout is a very important factor in advancing the design, and we ask for your cooperation in realizing the design with the layout you have in mind.

#4. Adjustments


Based on the finalized layout, we will propose design proposals and quotations. I will explain in detail using CG perspective and material samples. We aim for a well-balanced space that clears various conditions such as cost, layout, conductors, equipment, functions, and durability, and make adjustments until the customer is satisfied.

#5. Contract


After repeated meetings, if you are satisfied with our design and the amount offered, you will be contracted. From here, it will take about a week to prepare for the start of construction.

#6. Construction


Under the supervision of our on-site supervisor, we will proceed with the construction safely. At Largo, in order to undertake the entire process from design to construction, we will form a team consisting of construction managers, designers, and directors within the company and proceed with the construction under strict checks. In addition, before the start of construction, we will always put out the ink on the layout on the site and have the customer actually check the dimensions etc. at the site before starting the construction.

#7. Confirmation


After the construction starts, we will proceed while checking in detail such as quality check and schedule management. In addition, if there is a need for correction, we will promptly report it to the customer and make improvements based on confirmation.

#8. Display


Even if you design the interior, the space is not complete by itself. As long as we are particular about the original, we will not neglect the interior and will propose display items according to the situation. With or without this step, the impression of space makes a big difference. If you make a budget, we will carry out display construction that matches the atmosphere of the space.

#9. Delivery


A final check will be conducted in the presence of the customer to confirm that there are no problems with the dimensions, fit, or quality before delivery. We have a defect warranty period of one year even after delivery. If any construction defects or problems occur, we will respond free of charge for one year. This is because we will continue to have a long-term relationship as a customer's partner even after the construction is completed.
